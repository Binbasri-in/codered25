Frequently Asked Questions

What is a 25-hour hackathon?

+

A 25-hour hackathon is an intense coding competition where participants work on innovative projects within a 25-hour timeframe. It's an extra hour of creativity compared to traditional 24-hour hackathons!

Who can participate?

+

All students, developers, and tech enthusiasts are welcome! Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, there's a place for you in our hackathon.

What should I bring?

+

Bring your laptop, charger, any hardware you plan to use, and lots of enthusiasm! We'll provide food, drinks, and a comfortable hacking space.

Are there prizes?

+

Yes! We have exciting prizes for different categories including Best Overall Hack, Most Innovative Idea, and Best Beginner Hack. Prize details will be announced at the opening ceremony.

Do I need a team?

+

Teams can have up to 4 members. Don't have a team? No problem! We'll have team formation activities at the beginning of the event to help you find teammates.
