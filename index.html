FAQ

What is CODE RED?

+

CODE RED is a 24-hour hackathon where participants work on innovative solutions to real-world problems. It's an opportunity to collaborate, learn, and create something amazing in just one day.

Who can participate?

+

Any student currently enrolled in a college or university can participate. Both undergraduate and graduate students are welcome.

Do I need a team?

+

Teams can consist of 2-4 members. Don't have a team? No worries! We'll help you find teammates during our team formation event.

What should I bring?

+

Bring your laptop, charger, student ID, and enthusiasm! We'll provide food, drinks, and a comfortable hacking space.
